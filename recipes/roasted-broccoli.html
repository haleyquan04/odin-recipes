Roasted Broccoli

Description

This roasted broccoli recipe is great for the summer, and is incredibly easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400F.
  2. Rinse the head of broccoli and split into smaller pieces.
  3. Coat the broccoli with olive oil, lemon juice, and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Evenly spread the broccoli on a baking tray
  5. Roast the broccoli for 18-22 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
